My curious history

My history is full of turnarounds. During my law graduation, I became quite interested on coding and decided to apply for one of the best computer science university courses in Brazil (of UFSC). I was approved in their selection and started the graduation in parallel with law (yes...). Well, you can imagine that all hell broke loose. At the end of the day, I had to make a difficult decision - postponing my tech dream to finish law. 4 years later, living in Rio de Janeiro, I decided to resume my programming studies by myself and just some time after that I joined a new graduation - of system analysis -, in INFNET. The dream is alive! :)
